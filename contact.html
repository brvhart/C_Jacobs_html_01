DIRECTIONS

From the airport take the N2 direction Cape Town. Keeping left take the M3 direction Muizenberg( Left off ramp). Follow the M3 until the end then Turn left at the intersection.

At the next set of Traffic lights turn Right. Drive about 150m then Turn Right into Boyes Drive( mountain road). Follow Boyes Drive direction Kalk Bay. Take the 2nd Left into Anderson Road and then the 2nd left into Gatesville Road. Castle Hill is the big white building at the end on your left.

Kalk Bay is easy to get to whether by car or public transport. From the airport follow the N2 towards Cape Town and when you see an exit for the M3 southbound, take it. Follow the M3 into the M4 and from Muizenberg follow the coast road south past St. James to Kalk Bay.

Or if you prefer follow the scenic Boyes Drive from just before Muizenberg to Kalk Bay - This is an excellent location to whale spot from as well.
